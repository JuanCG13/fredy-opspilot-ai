# Demo script

## Flow 1 — Support refund triage

1. Open the dashboard.
2. Click `Trigger refund demo`.
3. Explain the intake: customer asks for refund after 19 days on Pro plan.
4. Show retrieved refund policy snippets.
5. Show classification: `Partial Refund Eligible`.
6. Edit the drafted response.
7. Approve or reject.
8. Show the run event log.

## Flow 2 — Lead qualification

1. Click `Lead Qualification`.
2. Show score and reasoning.
3. Show generated follow-up draft.
4. Explain how a real client could connect this to HubSpot, GoHighLevel, n8n, Make, or a custom CRM.

## Flow 3 — Internal policy Q&A

1. Click `Internal Policy Q&A`.
2. Show retrieved onboarding policy snippets.
3. Explain grounded answer + citations.
